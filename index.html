I received my Ph.D from the Department of Electrical and Computer Engineering at Clemson University in 2016. Before coming to Clemson, I received both my BS degree in Software Engineering and MS degree in Computer Engineering from Sun Yat-sen (Zhongshan) University, Guangzhou, China in 2009 and 2012 respectively.
